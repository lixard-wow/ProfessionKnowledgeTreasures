Profession Knowledge Tracker

Automatically tracks and routes you to every profession knowledge treasure in the Midnight expansion zones. Designed for players who want to max out their crafting professions efficiently without manually hunting down each collectible.

Features

  • Auto-detects your professions — only shows treasures relevant to what you have learned
  • Optimized route — uses nearest-neighbor + 2-opt path optimization to minimize travel time within each zone
  • Smart zone ordering — starts from wherever you are and continues in the most efficient zone order
  • Portal suggestions — tells you which portal to take when moving between zones, with a map pin placed directly on the portal
  • Auto-advances on loot — waypoint automatically moves to the next treasure after you collect one
  • Native WoW waypoints — places the blizzard waypoint pin and super-tracks it automatically
  • TomTom support — if TomTom is installed, waypoints are added there as well
  • Darkmoon Faire helper — built-in guide for the +2 knowledge quests available each month at the Darkmoon Faire, with vendor locations, required items, and step-by-step instructions
  • Phasing notes — items that are phase-locked or require quest completion are clearly labeled
  • Progress tracking — see remaining treasures broken down by profession at a glance

Supported Zones

  • Silvermoon City
  • Eversong Woods
  • Zul'Aman
  • Atal'Aman
  • Voidstorm / Slayer's Rise
  • Harandar / The Den

Supported Professions

Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Enchanting, Engineering, Herbalism, Inscription, Jewelcrafting, Leatherworking, Mining, Skinning, Tailoring

Commands

Type /pkt or /profknowledge to open or close the tracker window.

  • /pkt — toggle the tracker window
  • /pkt next — jump to the next treasure in the route
  • /pkt prev — go back to the previous treasure
  • /pkt first — jump to the first uncollected treasure
  • /pkt nearest — jump to the nearest treasure in your current zone
  • /pkt reload — rebuild the route (use after logging in or switching characters)
  • /pkt dmf — open the Darkmoon Faire knowledge quest helper
  • /pkt list — print all remaining treasures to chat
  • /pkt mapid — print your current map ID (for bug reporting)

How It Works

On login, the addon scans your professions and builds an optimized route through all uncollected treasures. It places a waypoint on the first item and automatically moves to the next after each loot. When you need to travel to a different zone, it identifies the correct portal and places the waypoint directly on it. The frame stays visible until all treasures are collected, and reappears on login if any remain.

Notes

  • Some items in Atal'Aman are phased and only visible after completing the local storyline
  • Some items require specific quest or achievement progress — notes are shown in the tracker when applicable
  • The Darkmoon Faire quests reset monthly and grant +2 knowledge per profession
